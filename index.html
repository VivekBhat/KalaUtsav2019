FAQ

What is the price of a food voucher

It is $11 per voucher

Where can I buy my food voucher?

You can buy online here. Please click on the Buy Food Vouchers tab on the top.

What do you mean by a food voucher?

The PayPal receipt (soft copy) you get when you purchase is considered a food voucher. This voucher gets you food at the event.

Do I need to get a printout of the receipt of the food voucher?

No. Only a soft copy of the PayPal receipt on your mobile or Tablet is needed as a proof of food voucher. Our volunteers will be checking for the PayPal receipt.

Do I need to buy food vouchers to attend the event?

Event admission is free of charge. No purchase is required for entry. Advance purchase of a food voucher is only required if you would like food from Biryani Corner at the event. All food vouchers must be purchased online prior to the event. No vouchers will be sold onsite.

Do you have any walk-in food voucher?

No. All food vouchers are available to purchase before the event.

Can I buy food voucher for my friends who is non-Intel?

Yes you can. We would ask you to restrict to friends and family. Please follow Intel's code of conduct/ethics policies.

Do I need to buy food vouchers for kids

Yes, and we offer free food for children 3 and under.

Can the food Voucher be refunded?

No. All sales are final and we do not issue any refunds.

Is parking included in the food voucher price?

No. Please plan ahead for parking. As the event is hosted in Downtown Portland nearer to MAX stations, we recommend you to use public transport or carpool with your friends and family.
